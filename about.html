About GLP-1 Care Companion

Your trusted source for evidence-based GLP-1 medication support

Our Mission

GLP-1 Care Companion was created to provide practical, evidence-based support for individuals using GLP-1 medications like Ozempic, Wegovy, and Mounjaro. We understand that managing side effects, optimizing nutrition, and maintaining muscle mass can be challenging, and we're here to help.

What We Offer

  • Educational Content: Evidence-based guides on nausea management, protein intake, hydration, and muscle preservation
  • Interactive Tools: Calculators for protein needs, hydration tracking, and meal planning
  • Practical Resources: Meal plans, recipes, and strategies that work with GLP-1 medications
  • Community Support: A platform to share experiences and learn from others

Our Values

  • Evidence-Based: All information is grounded in scientific research and medical best practices
  • Non-Medical: We provide education and support, not medical advice
  • Accessible: Free resources available to everyone
  • Respectful: We respect your healthcare decisions and privacy

Important Disclaimer

⚠️ Medical Disclaimer: This website provides educational information only. It is NOT a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your physician or qualified healthcare provider before making medical decisions or changing your medication regimen.
